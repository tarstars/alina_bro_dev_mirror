Bilingual signs

Hrazdan railway station

In Armenia, many signs are duplicated in Russian or English. This is a surprisingly good way to learn the Armenian alphabet. Today we want to share a photo taken at Hrazdan railway station. This place is not very popular among tourists, but it has its own charm. Later we will share more photos from this location.

Below is a mapping between the Armenian name ՀՐԱԶԴԱՆ and its Russian transliteration "razdan." You can see that the letter Հ (H) is not pronounced here, Ր corresponds to "R", Ա to "A", Զ to "Z", Դ to "D", the second Ա again to "A", and Ն to "N".

```text
Հ  Ր  Ա  Զ  Դ  Ա  Ն
   r  a  z  d  a  n
```
