Boo means owl!

Barev everyone!

In Armenian there is a word "բու" that means "owl" and is pronounced roughly as "boo." About a year ago we started noticing owl-themed ads on streets and in the metro - bright and memorable. Naturally, everyone loves owls; they instantly catch your eye.

Later we found out these charming funny characters advertise a brand that makes instant tea and coffee. I even bought a couple, mostly because I loved the packaging, but I still have not tried the taste, so we will not recommend anything yet.

But sharing these fun owl signs and packages - absolutely yes.
