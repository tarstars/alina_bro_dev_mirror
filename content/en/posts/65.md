Sevan. Part 2

As mentioned earlier, our 1.5-day trip was full of interesting places and activities. On the second day we found a diving club on the lake shore. Club "Altitude 1900" can be found [on Booking](<https://www.booking.com/Share-eAKyW1W>), [on the web](<https://diving.am/ru/dive-resort/>), or on [Google Maps](<https://maps.app.goo.gl/R47hhhHg7mjxbVLy9>). The club also provides accommodation: rooms are small but fine for an overnight stay. There is rental equipment for diving and SUP boards. We decided to rent SUPs, especially since the weather helped: sunny and windless. We had a pleasant 1.5-hour ride on a perfectly mirror-like lake 🌊.

Even during booking by phone, I was pleasantly surprised by the service. The club director, Armen, turned out to be a very friendly and welcoming person who answered all our questions with pleasure. Truly European-level service.

Another bonus: the company is a distributor of Scubapro products - clothes and accessories for water sports at wholesale prices. For reference, we had found a similar store in Moscow, and prices there were roughly 1.5x higher. We could not resist and bought a few items.

There is also a café on the territory with tasty snacks: sandwiches, fries, pancakes, coffee, and tea. Prices are absolutely reasonable. The place is designed in a bright, stylish way; photos attached.
