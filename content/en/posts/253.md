Non-Tourist Hrazdan. Loco Restaurant.

In Tsaghkadzor we already know all the tastiest cafes and restaurants. The task was to find a good place in Hrazdan. After checking reviews, we went for lunch at [Loco](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPAirC3D>). Right at the entrance we were surprised by the service quality and modern design across three floors - bright and contemporary.

We ordered several dishes. If you are curious, you can check the menu on their website - the real dishes match the photos: [https://loco.am/ru/menu](<https://loco.am/ru/menu>). They serve European, traditional Armenian, and even Asian cuisine. The surprising part: all of it was genuinely tasty. Kebabs, trout fillet, tom yum - all great. They also have beautiful and delicious desserts.

So far this place is top-tier for me. Prices are reasonable for this level of food, slightly cheaper than good places in Yerevan. Definitely recommended. Do you still believe Hrazdan is “non-tourist”?
