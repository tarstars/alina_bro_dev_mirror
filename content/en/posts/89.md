Competitive programming guides.

* [A. Shen: "Programming: theorems and problems"](<https://sch57.ru/files/pub/prog/Programming_theorem_and_problems.pdf>)
*[ Cormen Leiserson Rivest Stein: «Introduction to Algorithms» ](<https://matematika76.ru/fm/Кормен.pdf>)

Web:
* [max algo](<http://e-maxx.ru/algo/>) - static site with a lot of interesting and useful algorithms
* [sirius pythonic algo courses](<https://edu.sirius.online/#/course/2678>)
* [sirius c++ algo courses](<https://edu.sirius.online/#/course/2677>)
