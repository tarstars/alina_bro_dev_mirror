Shaki Waterfall. Devil's Bridge. Viewpoint

Even though we drove toward Tatev for just a day, we had a packed program.

After the cable car, the driver suggested driving up to the "Devil's Bridge". It's a natural bridge 30 meters long. Beneath the bridge hides a whole cascade of natural thermal pools and caves.

And then we stopped at the Shaki waterfall. It was Sunday, and there was a huge crowd. Including artists painting en plein air. Despite the crowds, we saw the waterfall, took photos, were impressed. The place is pleasant and picturesque - it's not for nothing that the artists gather there.

On the way back we stopped at a viewpoint with a delightful view. We met a funny family of dogs. The mother of the family climbed up with us to a beautiful arch and shared a snack with a view of the mountains. The dog definitely has good taste.

After that we drove back and once again enjoyed the road through the Syunik region.
