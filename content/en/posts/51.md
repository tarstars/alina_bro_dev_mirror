What to do on the weekend

Sevan. Tsovasar

[A cozy corner](<https://maps.app.goo.gl/w8jqdUMyL5WW8AAr8>) for a family vacation away from the highway, on the shore of the lake locals call a sea. The window view opens to mountains. They are never the same. The lake changes even more: different color shades during the day, smooth in the morning, and by evening a storm with waves reaching almost to the dining hall windows.

The territory is designed with a lot of imagination. There is a dining hall styled like a Viking ship, a mini-kitchen for barbecue, branded benches with the Tsovasar name, several stairways going down to the water, and a swimming pool. The pool is heated, but in September the heating is already not fully enough.
