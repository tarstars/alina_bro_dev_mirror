Cultural program: "5 days in Yerevan"

Barev, everyone!

Here is a small plan, already successfully tested, for how to entertain friends during five full days in Yerevan.

1. Visit the Yerevan Opera Theatre. Ticket prices are very reasonable; good orchestra seats cost 8000-10000 dram. We chose Puccini’s *La Boheme*; we wrote about our impressions [here](<https://t.me/alina_yerevan_js/313>)

2. Take a trip out of town. We chose Sevan. Sit in [U Semo](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4B6lx>) or [Yasaman](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4BNKw>) with a lake view and try Sevan whitefish. Yasaman is more atmospheric and refined. U Semo has unique finely shredded potatoes. Both places are top. Prices are quite reasonable for this level.

3. Visit a museum.
We chose the [Martiros Saryan Museum](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4B8~X>):
- it was open on Monday
- a compact two-floor exhibition plus memorial rooms, which mattered to us because we had a one-year-old child with us
- a chance to recharge with bright colors in the cold spring

4. Walk along Cascade. Look at the [sculptures](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4B21V>) and the city from the [viewpoint](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4FJo2>)

5. Ponchik-Monchik: eat delicious Gyumri doughnuts [in the center](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4F6IW>); [more details](<https://t.me/alina_yerevan_js/183>)

6. Sherep. Eat traditional Armenian dishes [in a good restaurant](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4F8lJ>).

7. Walk around the center. [Northern Avenue](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4JWPC>), Saryan Street, etc.; stop by [Vernissage](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4JP9T>) to browse souvenirs and antiques.

8. Blue Mosque: go [inside the grounds](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4NAiK>) (in summer it is absolutely wonderful), look at the hordes of cats, and take a "the cats have landed" photo.

9. Arabkir:
👉 Walk along Komitas to the small [Prague Park](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4RBoV>)
👉 Eat something tasty at the [SAS food court](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4RVOr>)
👉 Look at Willy’s little house next to the SAS store
👉 Walk to [Nor Arabkir Park](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4VBIc>) and admire the city view
👉 Eat [delicious shawarma](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4VS3B>)

10. [View](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPB4VX1c>) of the Hrazdan Gorge from Davtashen Bridge
