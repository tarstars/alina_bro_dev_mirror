Yerevan. Walk Through Arabkir.

Today is an absolutely amazing day. Sunny and spring-warm, especially in contrast with snowy Moscow and its “snow ridges” in every courtyard.

We walked from Moskovyan through Baghramyan and Komitas, then via Prague Park to Eraz. On Baghramyan, one mandatory stop is Lurik (the owl in the pharmacy) - we already [wrote about her](<https://t.me/alina_yerevan_js/25>). We also entered the modern Surb Khach (Holy Cross) church - huge and very minimalist. Then we visited our favorite cozy cafe, Altar. Photos from the walk are attached.

Bonus: a view of Mount Araler.
