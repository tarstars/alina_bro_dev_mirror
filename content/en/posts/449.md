Monday. Arabkir. Pastry shop. A mouse

We've all caught some bug and are trying to recover. But nobody cancelled our errands. So after work we walked the route: pet store (for a mouse for our snake) - grocery store - pastry shop.

Our friends have lived here for more than four years now, and they used to bring a cake to our get-togethers. We're not big fans of cakes, but these were somehow special. Not cloying, and made from natural ingredients, with butter in the list. That's a rarity: when you read the ingredients of sweets, something like that is hard to find. We knew this pastry shop was somewhere in our area, but we hadn't looked in - and now we did.

So, the pastry shop looks very modest, tucked in the courtyards of Papazyan. There's a big selection of cookies and cakes. Cakes are sold both by the slice and whole. Photos attached. One of them shows the cake our friend ordered for our Halloween party.

📌 [Address of the Anare pastry shop](<https://yandex.com/maps/-/CPWb4U5x>)
