Tsaghkadzor. Beginning.

Barev dzez.

It has been a while since we talked about places in Armenia, so we are fixing that. The next post cycle will be about Tsaghkadzor.

A beautiful mountain town just one hour from Yerevan. In summer it is scenic and peaceful. In winter it turns into a popular ski resort with cute cafes and restaurants. It is quite small but very cozy.

So, in summer we decided to rent a hotel and live in this town for a while. No big plans: walk around, breathe mountain air, take the ski lift, and walk on top. And then, as we approach the hotel, we see them - huge striped insects the size of obese locusts, everywhere, persistently trying to throw themselves under your feet. For me: total "brrr." Arseniy liked them and caught them. As we later understood, our trip would be closely connected with Tsaghkadzor wildlife... coming soon.
