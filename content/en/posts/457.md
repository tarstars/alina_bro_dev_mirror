Armenia. Jermuk part 1

Yesterday was our moving day to the resort town of Jermuk.
The road from Yerevan takes almost 3 hours, but it's so scenic that you don't get tired. We saw storks with chicks and in flight. Mountain birds of prey - golden eagles and small eagles - over the gorges.
In Jermuk it's still quite cool right now, and there's snow in places, while the trees are still bare, without leaves.
Jermuk is a resort town with lots of hotels and sanatoriums.
Nearby, in the center, there's a gallery of mineral waters, which we walked over to.
In the evening, in the soft evening light, the town becomes even cozier and warmer.
