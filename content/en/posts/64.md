What to do on the weekend

Sevan. Tsovasar.

Final post about the Sea Mountain.

There is a cozy restaurant on the property where you can have breakfast (if it is included with your stay) and also grab food any time.

A round hall with a fireplace in the center, pleasant service. We especially liked khashlama and grilled Sevan whitefish.

The desserts were impressive: baklava and a "Drunken Cherry" sponge cake. It was the most "drunken" cherry cake we have ever tried.
