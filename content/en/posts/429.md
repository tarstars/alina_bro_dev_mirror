Peace. Labour. May. The Yerevan Zoo.

Yesterday the weather was really great and sunny, and we finally went somewhere together. We love the Yerevan Zoo. Every year we try to get out to it. There was a huge crowd yesterday, but we accomplished the minimum program.

We fed a vulture an apple (we were very surprised) and ravens some carrot (they're unbelievably beautiful and huge). We watched a hippo open its mouth wide and saw an elephant carefully gathering carrots and apples off the floor.

We dropped into the exoterrarium and met a wonderful keeper. In the exoterrarium there are lots of beautiful snakes, caimans, various little lizards and iguanas. And bats too.
There's a pleasant summer café with corn and ice cream and even some kind of street food.
The grounds are large and cozy. A walk like this is sure to please both children and adults.
We definitely recommend it.
