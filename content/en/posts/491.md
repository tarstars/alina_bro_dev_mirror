Jermuk part 2. Waterfall.

In Jermuk itself there's a very beautiful waterfall, and the road to it is simply incredible: cliffs, mountain streams, a turbulent river.
Even though it wasn't so green at this time, the bare trees created some kind of unique, transparent look. I forgot to mention the eagles soaring over the gorge.

First we reached the first point, the "Arch of Wishes" - there's also a beautiful small waterfall here and a winding path to it. Then we came up to the most touristy spot, with the usual souvenirs and various treats: honey, herbs, churchkhela. And after that we saw it: a huge, turbulent mass of water running down from the mountain.

We're attaching photos of the waterfall and the road, as well as the souvenir stall with various treats.
