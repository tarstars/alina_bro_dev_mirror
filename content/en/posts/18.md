Introduction and Overview of This Blog.

This channel is about:
✍️ life after relocation to Yerevan
✍️ travel tips for Armenia
✍️ adventures in learning HTML, CSS, JS, and Python
✍️ moving to another country and studying frontend development, [GitHub profile](<https://github.com/AlinaBrode>)

About the author

My name is Alina. I am married and have a teenage son. In my previous career, I worked as an accountant and a methodology specialist in accounting and tax reporting. I love drawing and enjoy playing percussion. For many years I worked in large industrial companies. I still consider myself an energy-industry person, because I spent almost 14 years in this field and saw the power of power plants with my own eyes.

Turning point

The years 2020-2023 became a turning point for me: from an ordinary Moscow resident I turned into an adventurer. Leaving behind our small familiar world with our own apartment, stable job, financial cushion, and established routine, we moved to Armenia. My husband transferred to a local hub, I left my job, and became the classic "IT specialist's wife."

Blog

Here you will find notes about:
✍️ tourist and non-tourist Armenia: where to go, what to see, and what impressed me
✍️ the polished side of Yerevan and its hidden corners
✍️ the difficult path of retraining through an online school and self-learning in frontend development and programming basics
✍️ learning a new profession as a prompt engineer
✍️ relocation preparation, school search, apartment search, and many small but important details that are easy to overlook
