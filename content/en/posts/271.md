Reflections in the Net

Hi everyone!

🌍 Since 2020, the world has changed so much that Yerevan has become a kind of hub city where friends scattered across different locations can gather. In that sense it is like Lenin Library: equally convenient for everyone, or equally inconvenient.

🤝 We have such a meetup planned for early March. We’ve started slowly designing where to go and how to organize everything. Travel planning also needs to account for the group: adults, a teenager, and a one-year-old child.

So far, we bought tickets to Yerevan Opera for Puccini’s *La Bohème* - classic Italian with Armenian subtitles, but that won’t stop us. We’ll definitely share updates as we go.
