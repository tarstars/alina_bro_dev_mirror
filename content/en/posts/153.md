🌲Pre-New-Year Yerevan

☃️“New Year, New Year. New Year is everywhere! The old year was already treated as if it were dead; its property was sold cheaply, like the belongings of a drowned sailor on a ship. It was still breathing, but its fashions had already become last year’s and were sold for next to nothing. Its treasures turned to dust compared with the riches of its unborn successor.”
Charles Dickens

❄️ So what am I getting at? Merry Christmas and Jingle Bells are everywhere. At this time of year, Yerevan turns into an extraordinary Christmas box. And yes, opening one day of an advent calendar has now become popular, and we decided to dedicate this day - don’t be surprised - to the mannequins in Yerevan stores.

❄️ This is an unforgettable Yerevan vibe, quite comparable to King’s aesthetics. It is incredibly hard to catch all the unique works of these contemporary artists: every seller tries to surprise with their own distinctive finds.

👻 So, let’s open our advent calendar for 13.12 and enjoy.
